## Modifications

In the video, we opted for farm-style background music along with many funny and some real life sound effects. Mostly for every part of the video where the entities are doing any type of action, we put sound effects from the videogame Minecraft, a few from meme audios and a few real life audios like saws cutting metal and falling pipes.

## Justification

We chose for a mix of a funny and farm like theme audio, combining meme sound effects aswell as real life effects to let the listener or viewer think everything else but a psy-fi space third person shooter roguelike.
